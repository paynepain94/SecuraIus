Ralf Schumacher

Former Formula 1 driver, Entrepreneur, TV Expert

Ralf Schumacher is one of Germanys most iconic motorsport figures, known for shaping a golden era of Formula 1. With six Grand Prix victories and 27 podiums over an 11-year F1 career, he brought precision, resilience, and strategy to the worlds most elite racing teams, including Williams and Toyota. His journey began on his familys karting track in Kerpen, where a passion for speed turned into a lifelong pursuit of performance.

Today, Schumacher brings that same drive to the future of motorsportas a Sky Sports Germany pundit, team mentor, and entrepreneur. From pioneering hybrid technologies and sustainable fuels to exploring the role of artificial intelligence in racing, he is at the forefront of innovation where man and machine meet. His focus: translating the split-second decisions, data-driven precision, and relentless teamwork of Formula 1 into powerful lessons for business, leadership, and human potential.

Florence Gaub

Leading Military Strategist and Futurologist, Director of the Research Division at NATO Defence College

Dr. Florence Gaub is one of Europes foremost thinkers on future conflict, global security, and geopolitical transformation. As Director of Research at the NATO Defence College in Rome, she helps shape strategic foresight at the highest levelsadvising governments and international bodies with scenario-based insights. A political scientist by training and former Deputy Director of the EU Institute for Security Studies, Gaub has served as a reserve officer in the French Army, analyzed post-war Middle East dynamics, and led the World Economic Forums Future Council on Frontier Risks. Her mission: to make tomorrow’s threats visible today.

Alexis Conran

Actor, Writer, and TV Presenter

Alexis Conran is a renowned TV presenter, actor, and expert in scams and social engineering, best known for uncovering the psychological tricks used by fraudsters. As the face of the BBCs The Real Hustle and host on Times Radio, Alexis reveals how even the savviest individuals can be manipulated into risky behavior.

At HuFiCon, he brings a unique blend of storytelling, real-world examples, and deep insight into human behavior helping security professionals better understand the tactics of deception and how to build stronger human defenses in the face of growing social engineering threats.

Shannon Jaritz

VP Customer Success, SoSafe

Shannon Jaritz is Vice President of Customer Success at SoSafe, where she leads the teams responsible for guiding customers from onboarding and integration through to long-term satisfaction and retention. Her entire career has been dedicated to putting customers first, and she brings a data-driven mindset to building strong, lasting relationships that drive value. Shannon firmly believes that customers are a companys greatest asset a principle she and her team champion every day at SoSafe. Beyond her role, Shannon has also been the wonderful moderator of HuFiCon for two editions, helping shape meaningful conversations and connect the community around people-centric cybersecurity.

Dr. Anne Meckbach

Chief Compliance Officer, BESTSECRET

Dr. Anne Meckbach is the Chief Compliance Officer of BESTSECRET Group. She is responsible, among other things, for the group-wide management and strategic development of the areas of compliance, risk management, and sustainability for BESTSECRET. Previously, she served as Chief Compliance Officer at ProSiebenSat.1 Media SE. Before transitioning into the compliance field, she worked both in-house and as a lawyer at an international law firm specializing in corporate law and M&A.

Dr. Niklas Hellemann

Co-founder and CEO, SoSafe

Niklas Hellemannis a studied psychologist, social engineering expert, and Co-founder & CEO at SoSafe. Before founding his own company,heworked as a management consultant atthe Boston Consulting Groupforsixyears in the areas of People and Organization. Besides his Ph.D. in Business Administration, Niklas studied psychology which led him to the human approach in the tech-savvy cyber security market. In 2018, Niklas founded SoSafe together with Lukas and Felix to strengthen digital self-defense worldwide. Today, Niklas is engaged as a speaker on various stages worldwide, including the OMR Festival, the Web Summit, and the RSA Conference, where he talks about the pressing challenges of social engineering and his learnings as the CEO of a B2B scale-up.

Charline Kappes

Program Manager Public, SoSafe

Charline Kappes is Program Manager Public at SoSafe, where she drives awareness for people-centric cybersecurity in the public sector. With over five years at SoSafe and training as an Information Security Officer, she combines strategic program work with deep knowledge of security frameworks and compliance. As a host of the Human Firewall Podcast, Charline breaks down complex cybersecurity topics for broad audiences. A sought-after speaker known for her engaging presence, she regularly addresses cybercrime and the human factor in security. Outside of work, she is active in politics, advocating for digital resilience and cybersecurity education.

Marian Schneider

Vice President IT Security, BESTSECRET

Marian Schneider is a cybersecurity expert with a passion for building modern, user-focused security strategies. Since 2017, he has held a range of technical and leadership roles in multinational companies, including work as a penetration tester.

With deep experience in creating lean, forward-thinking security frameworks, Marian has been leading the Information and Physical Security teams at BESTSECRET since January 2022. There, he is driving the development of a highly automated, efficient in-house security department. His focus: leveraging cutting-edge technologies and smart processes to deliver sustainable, practical security solutions that empower users and protect organizations.
